What is the "Deca Steam Secret"? Why am I being asked for it?

It's not your Steam password. It's a long-lived token Deca created for you the first time you linked your Steam account to RotMG Exalt. Their launcher caches it locally so you only ever go through Steam once.

Fastest way to get it: if you've launched RotMG via Steam on this PC at least once, the secret is already saved. Just click 📥 Import from Launcher below and we'll read it out of the launcher's settings (HKCU\\Software\\DECA Live Operations GmbH\\RotMG Exalt Launcher\\Productionps) and base64-decode it for you.

Never used the launcher on this PC? You'll need to run RotMG Exalt via Steam once (the launcher contacts Deca, gets a fresh secret, and caches it). Then come back and click Import.

Have multiple accounts? Sign each one into the launcher in turn — our injected DLL captures every login. Then click 📥 Import All Captured to add them all at once.

Welcome to Realm Engine

      
Your all-in-one companion for Realm of the Mad God. Let's take a quick tour so you know where everything is.

      
This will only take a minute.

    


    
    

      
1 / 4

      
Home

      
The Home tab is your command center. From here you can:

      
    
        
  • Active Script — Quick link to the Scripts tab for disk-based .js scripts.
    • 
        
  • Session Stats — Track uptime, fame gained, white bags, events killed, and dungeons completed in real time.
    • 
        
  • Accounts Ready — Quickly launch any of your saved accounts directly from the dashboard.
    • 
        
  • Edit Layout — Click the pencil icon in the top corner to rearrange, hide, or restore cards. Drag to reorder and save your custom layout.
    • 
      

    


    
    

      
2 / 4

      
Plugins

      
Plugins extend what Realm Engine can do. The plugin hub lets you:

      
    
        
  • Browse & Search — Find plugins by name or filter by category.
    • 
        
  • Enable / Disable — Toggle plugins on and off with a single click.
    • 
        
  • Configure — Each plugin has its own settings panel — tweak them to fit your playstyle.
    • 
      

      
Plugins include utilities like auto-aim, auto-dodge, auto-nexus and more.

    


    
    

      
3 / 4

      
Accounts

      
This is where you manage your Realm accounts. You can store multiple accounts, view character overviews, vault contents, and launch into the game.

      
Let's add your first account now to get you started.

      

        

          
          
        

        

          
          
        

      

      

      
You can always add more accounts later or skip this step for now.

    


    
    

      
4 / 4

      
Damage Sniffer

      
The Damage Sniffer records combat data while you play. It tracks:

      
    
        
  • Runs — Each encounter is logged with a live view and saved history.
    • 
        
  • Targets — See every enemy you hit, filter by boss or miniboss.
    • 
        
  • Player Breakdown — View per-player DPS and damage contributions.
    • 
      

      
Data is captured automatically — just play and review your stats anytime.

    


    
    

      
      
You're all set!

      
That covers the essentials. You can explore each tab at your own pace. If you ever need to revisit this tour, you can reset it in Settings.
